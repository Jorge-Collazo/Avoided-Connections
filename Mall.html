AVOIDED CONNECTION - MALL

Stefan Ekelöf:


Sent at 10:49PM on Monday, June 17


Please watch your children when you’re at a crowded mall. This weekend, I was trying to buy my 6 year old son some candy, and he walked away to find a table in the food court while I fumbled with my wallet, trying to pay the vendor. When I turned to look for my son, I found him 50 yards away, walking hand in hand with a guy in a clown costume, I had to run and hold my son away from him. While the clown ran out of the mall.
